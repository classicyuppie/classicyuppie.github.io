IndyGeek.net

Started in 2009, IndyGeek.net provided reporting and commentary on all things technology throughout Indianapolis. During its life, IG produced spectacular coverage of Central Indiana's most prized events: GenCon, Circle of Lights, and Blog Indiana to name a few. IndyGeek.net ceased publication in 2011 shortly before its second anniversary.

A fully-functional archive of the site can be found at indygeekarchive.com
